Cheesy Baked Ziti Recipe

Easy Cheesy Baked Ziti

Baked Ziti is a simple and delicious baked pasta dish that never fails to please. This easy baked ziti recipe is extra creamy and cheesy! Everything bakes together into something so tasty…the best comfort food recipe ever!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked ziti
  • 1 32 ounce jar Marinara sauce
  • 1 8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  2. Heat the marinara sauce and cream cheese in a saucepan, combining them into one sauce.
  3. Add the pasta into the pot of sauce and stir it all together, so that the ziti is well covered.
  4. Pour half of the ziti & sauce into the prepared casserole dish, then sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Pour the rest of the ziti into the dish and top with the rest of the mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Cover the casserole dish and bake for 30 minutes at 375°F. Then remove the lid, and bake uncovered for another 15-20 minutes.
