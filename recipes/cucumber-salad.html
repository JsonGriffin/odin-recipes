Quick and Easy Cucumber Salad Recipe

This Cucumber Salad Recipe comes together quickly and with just a few ingredients. A refreshing and tangy side salad itâ€™s a great accompaniment to chicken, grilled meat, seafood and so much more!

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 1 red onion sliced
  • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice each cucumber in 1/4 inch slices and add to a large bowl.
  2. Add in the sliced red onion (sliced into half moonand toss to combine.
  3. In a mason jar or small bowl whisk together the apple cider vinegar, water, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing mixture over the cucumber and onion and toss to fully coat/combine.
  5. Keep in the fridge until ready to serve.
  6. Enjoy!
